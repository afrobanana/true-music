Ballantine’s is committed to making
authentic connections and bringing people together.

One way this is done is by taking a stand for True Music, and that means exclusively supporting the artists that create from a place of true passion.

Ballantine’s True Music Cyprus is all about delving into the alternative dance music culture on the island, with the simple mission to uncover, celebrate and showcase the most genuine music scenes and stories currently making waves here in Cyprus. We are supporting True Music artists through Showcase Events, through active conversations where they tell their stories and talk about their passion and now through this map.

This map showcases True Music artists’ place on the local scene. It will be updated regularly so keep coming back!
